		
		Mini Vmac 3.3.2 LIBRETRO DEBUG VERSION

Compile:

	Go to jni directory, and type ndk-build .

 
Setup:

	- First , create a folder on /mnt/sdcard/vmac/ .
	- Put a MacIIx.ROM and some DSK/IMG files in this folder.


Usage:

	Launch retroarch , select a startup dsk , and mini vmac sould boot.
